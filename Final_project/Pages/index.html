About Us

Coding is like Maths. Learning theory or watching videos is not enough. You have to practise solving problems, work on areas of weakness and build topic level mastery to gain the confidence you need to tackle real world problems head on. Therefore, Coding Ninjas has come up with a unique '3-Stage-Learning-Model'. In the Learn Stage, we offer a seamless learning experience with lectures, problem solving modules and projects. We gamify the whole learning experience through leaderboards and awards to keep it engaging and fun. You can effortlessly track your coding journey and the progress you’ve made week by week, mapping your path to success. If you ever need extra time to catch up, no worries, our platform adapts to your pace, making learning stress-free. In the Excel Stage, basis the progress you’ve made in the Learn Stage, we help you identify areas of improvement at a topic level and give you additional resources to bridge your skill gaps so that you can excel in your chosen field of interest. In the Standout Stage, we help you stand out with recruiters on platforms like Naukri, by building a coding profile on Code 360 through participation in weekly coding contents, events and so on. We also give you personalised feedback and interview insights. By the time you graduate, you would not just be a coder. You’d be a Coding Ninja!
